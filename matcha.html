    1. Café Kitsune

    A Stylish Haven for the Matcha lovers

    Café Kitsuné, a renowned French-Japanese brand, has established itself as a popular destination for Londoners seeking a stylish and sophisticated café experience. With its minimalist aesthetic, delectable pastries, expertly crafted coffee, and lets not forget Japanese graded quality matcha! It's no wonder this trendy spot has garnered rave reviews.

    The Matcha experience

    The matcha latte is a must-try, with its creamy texture and subtle sweetness. The presentation is chef's kiss—served in a sleek ceramic cup that's almost too pretty to drink. But trust me, the taste is just as satisfying as the aesthetics.

    If you're feeling adventurous, try the matcha affogato, a decadent combination of matcha ice cream and espresso. It's the perfect balance of sweet and bitter, with a rich and creamy texture. And let's not forget the matcha pastries—they're absolutely to die for. So, grab your sunglasses, a good book, and head to Café Kitsune for a brat summer experience that's both delicious and stylish.

    2. JENKI

    The Ultimate Brat Summer Sip

    Jenki Matcha is the it drink for London's brat summer. This trendy spot offers a variety of matcha-based beverages that are as delicious as they are Instagram-worthy.

    The Matcha Latte is a classic for a reason. It's creamy, slightly sweet, and has a beautiful emerald green hue that's perfect for your summer feed. But if you're feeling a little more adventurous, try the Matcha Espresso Tonic. It's a refreshing twist on the classic espresso tonic, with a subtle hint of matcha that adds a unique flavor profile.

    These treats are the perfect complement to your matcha and will satisfy your sweet tooth without being overly indulgent. So, grab your sunglasses, a good book, and head to Jenki Matcha for a brat summer experience that's both delicious and stylish.

    As of September 2024, JENKI Matcha has three locations in London:

    Spitalfields: This is their flagship location, offering a wide range of matcha-based drinks and treats.
    Selfridges: You can find JENKI Matcha within the Selfridges department store on Oxford Street.
    Covent Garden: Their newest location is in the heart of Covent Garden.

    3. Tsujiri Tea House

    Tsujiri Tea House

    Tsujiri Tea House is a must-visit for any matcha lover in London. This Japanese tea specialist offers a wide range of matcha-based drinks and desserts that are perfect for a brat summer afternoon.

    Matcha Iced Latte: This classic is a must-try for any matcha lover. The creamy texture and subtle sweetness of the matcha are perfectly balanced by the cold milk, making it the perfect refreshing drink for a hot summer day.

    Matcha Cheese Cake: If you're looking for a decadent treat, look no further than Tsujiri's matcha cheese cake. The rich, creamy texture of the cheese cake is complemented by the earthy flavor of the matcha, creating a truly indulgent dessert

    Matcha Ice Cream: For a lighter option, try Tsujiri's matcha ice cream. The smooth, creamy texture and vibrant green color make it a visually appealing and delicious treat.
