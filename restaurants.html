1. Ixchel

A Taste of Mexico in London

Ixchel is a vibrant Mexican restaurant in London that brings the flavors of Mexico to the heart of the city. With its authentic cuisine, colorful decor, and lively atmosphere, Ixchel is a must-visit for any food lover.

Authentic Mexican cuisine

Ixchel's menu features a wide variety of traditional Mexican dishes, from classic tacos and enchiladas to more adventurous options like mole and pozole. The chefs use fresh, high-quality ingredients to create dishes that are both flavorful and authentic.

Tequila o'Clock

No Mexican restaurant would be complete without a selection of delicious cocktails and tequila. Ixchel offers a wide range of options, from classic margaritas to more creative concoctions. Their tequila selection is extensive, featuring some of the finest tequilas from Mexico.

If you're looking for a taste of Mexico in London, Ixchel is a must-visit. With its authentic cuisine, vibrant atmosphere, and delicious cocktails, it's the perfect place to enjoy a memorable meal.

2. Alley Cat Pizza

A Slice of New York in London

Alley Cat Pizza just moved in the neighbourhood bringing the flavors of New York City straight to your doorstep. With their authentic Neapolitan-style pizzas, made with fresh, high-quality ingredients, Alley Cat Pizza has quickly become a local favorite.

Every bite of NYC

Alley Cat Pizza's pizzas are crafted with the same care and attention to detail as the best pizzerias in New York City. The dough is made with a special blend of flour, water, and yeast, resulting in a perfectly chewy and crispy crust. The toppings are fresh and flavorful, from classic combinations like pepperoni and mushroom to more adventurous options like spicy chorizo and pineapple

If you're a pizza lover, Alley Cat Pizza is a must-visit. With their authentic Neapolitan-style pizzas, fresh ingredients, and inviting atmosphere, it's no wonder why this hidden gem has become so popular. So, grab a slice and experience the taste of New York in London.

3. Singapulah

Singapulah is a vibrant and authentic Singaporean restaurant nestled in the heart of London's Chinatown. This bustling eatery offers a delicious array of dishes that capture the essence of Singaporean cuisine. From flavorful laksa to fragrant satay, Singapulah delivers a truly unforgettable dining experience.

A Culinary Adventure

Step into Singapulah and be transported to the vibrant streets of Singapore. The restaurant's lively atmosphere and colorful decor create a welcoming and inviting space. The menu is filled with traditional Singaporean dishes, each one bursting with flavor and authenticity.

Must-Try dishes

Laksa: A spicy noodle soup with coconut milk, seafood, and vegetables.
Satay: Tender skewers of grilled meat or tofu served with a peanut sauce.
Crab: A fiery dish featuring crab cooked in a spicy tomato-based sauce.
Hainanese Chicken Rice: A classic Singaporean dish consisting of steamed chicken served with fragrant rice and chili sauce.

Teleport to Singapore

Whether you're a seasoned fan of Singaporean cuisine or simply looking to explore new flavors, Singapulah is a must-visit. With its authentic dishes, vibrant atmosphere, and friendly service, this restaurant offers a truly memorable dining experience.
